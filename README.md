# Privacy Policy


**Call a Satellite- Mobile App**
Last updated: March 20, 2026
---

## 1. Information We Collect


### Device Permissions


- **Microphone & Speech Recognition** — Used solely for voice-to-text during satellite calls. Audio is processed on-device using Apple Speech Recognition or Google Speech Services. Raw audio is not stored or transmitted to our servers.
- **Location** — Used to calculate when the ISS and other satellites will pass over your area. Your coordinates are processed locally on-device for pass predictions and are not stored on our servers.
- **Notifications** — Used to alert you before a visible ISS pass. Notification preferences are stored locally on your device.


### Usage Data


We may collect anonymous, non-identifiable usage data to improve the service, including:

- Randomly generated session identifiers
- Question topics (with personal information removed)
- Response times and error rates


We do not collect or store personally identifiable information for analytics. Traffic sources may be categorized (e.g., "social media") but full URLs are not recorded.


### Account Data

If you create an account, we store your email address for authentication and account management purposes only.


---


## 2. How We Use Your Data


- **Voice input** is converted to text on-device and sent to our API to generate a satellite response. We do not retain transcripts beyond the active session.
- **Location data** is used locally to compute satellite pass predictions. It is not uploaded to any server.
- **Anonymous usage data** is used solely to improve product quality, fix issues, and understand how features are used.
- **Account data** is used only for authentication and managing your subscription.


---


## 3. Third-Party Services


We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:


- **Supabase** — Authentication and account management
- **OpenAI** — AI-generated satellite responses and text-to-speech
- **ElevenLabs** — Alternative voice synthesis
- **CelesTrak** — Public satellite orbital data (TLE)
- **Apple / Google Speech Services** — On-device speech recognition


We do not sell your personal information to any third party.


---


## 4. Data Storage & Security


- Account data is stored securely via Supabase with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest.
- Voice and location data are processed locally on your device and are not persisted on our servers.
- App preferences (voice mode, notification settings) are stored locally on your device using on-device storage.


---


## 5. Children's Privacy


Call a Satellite is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.


---


## 6. Your Rights


- You can revoke microphone, location, and notification permissions at any time in your device's Settings.
- You can sign out at any time to end your session.
- If you have an account, you may request deletion of your account data by contacting us at the email below.
